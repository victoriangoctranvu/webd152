My favorite beverage is boba milk tea. When brewed properly, a good boba milk tea is both creamy and subtly sweet with the tea at the forefront of the flavor profile. The boba itself plays a huge role into the enjoyment of the drink, and is one of the main reasons why I enjoy boba milk tea. Fresh boba pearls are warm, chewy, and slightly sweet with a hint of brown sugar. The drink is versatile, and can also serve as a dessert.
